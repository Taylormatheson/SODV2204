Adrianne Pieczonka


Adrianne Pieczonka is a candaian soprano who has sung across the world. The dramatic Soprano went to school in Ontario, at the University of Toronto and at Western University

In 2019 she was appointed "Chair in Voice" at the Glenn Gould School, Royal Conservatory of Music where she gives regular masterclasses, and oversees the vocal department and their opera productions. (wikipedia)

She has released a CD 'Adrianne Piecczonka Sings Strauss and Wagner' and her regordings of puccini arias won a JUNO award, and was named disk of the year/opera award by BBC magazine. Her album 'Beethoven: Ideals o the French Revolutionnnnn' also won a juno. Her performances of Elektra, Simon Boccanegra, Der Rosenkavalier, and DonGiovanni have all be immortalized on DVD.
